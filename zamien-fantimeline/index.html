Lil Uzi's Timeline

Description: The objective of this project is to give five quick facts about the goat Lil Uzi.

Timeline:

1. In the year 2015, Lil Uzi gained a lot of fame in the music industry with his third mixtape "Luv is rage" which was released on Octover 30, 2015.

2. In 2016,, Uzi released his next album "Lil Uzi Vert Vs. The World".

3. On June 13, 2016, Uzi was named as one of 2016's ten XXL Freshmen.

4. A month later, on his 22nd birthday (July 31), Uzi released his fifth mixtape called "The Perfect LUV Tape".

5. Topping off a successful 2016 year, Uzi was featured on Migos' song "Bad and Boujee" which reached Billboard's Hot 100.
